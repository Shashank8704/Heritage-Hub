Our Story

ThreadCraft was born from a simple belief: the people who create beautiful things deserve to be paid fairly for their craft.

The Problem We're Solving

India's handloom industry supports over 4.3 million weavers, yet most live below the poverty line. The traditional supply chain involves 4–6 middlemen between the weaver and the customer, each taking a cut. A saree that a weaver sells for ₹2,000 often retails for ₹15,000+.
